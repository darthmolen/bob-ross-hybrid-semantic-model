## 1. Composition
- Majestic snow-capped mountain dominates the center background, rising dramatically against a cloudy sky
- Winding blue stream flows from the mountain's base through the valley toward the foreground, creating a natural pathway
- Dark evergreen trees frame both left and right sides, acting as vertical anchors and natural borders
- Lush green meadows and grassy banks embrace the stream in the foreground and midground
- Soft clouds drift across a blue-gray sky, adding atmospheric depth
- Mist or atmospheric haze surrounds the mountain base, enhancing distance
- Classic triadic balance: dark trees on sides, bright mountain center, stream as vertical corridor
composition_archetype: **Alpine Valley Stream Corridor**

## 2. Palette
- **Prussian Blue** and **Phthalo Blue**: Sky gradations, water body, and reflective stream surface
- **Titanium White**: Snow-capped mountain peaks, cloud formations, and highlight accents
- **Midnight Black**: Base for dark evergreen trees and deep shadows
- **Sap Green**: Vibrant meadow grasses and midground vegetation
- **Phthalo Green**: Bright grass highlights and lush foreground foliage
- **Van Dyke Brown**: Tree trunks, earth tones, and warm shadow undertones
- **Yellow Ochre**: Golden grass highlights and sunlit meadow patches
- **Alizarin Crimson**: Subtle mountain shadow depths and cool-warm transitions
Palette identity: **Cool Mountain Serenity with Verdant Meadow Contrast**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Serene and tranquil wilderness sanctuary evoking peace and solitude
- Cool color dominance creates a calm, refreshing atmospheric quality
- Dramatic mountain peak provides grandeur and majesty without overwhelming the scene
- Soft, diffused light suggests overcast day or gentle morning illumination
- Inviting and accessible through the stream's meandering path
- Misty atmospheric perspective enhances depth and distance
- Timeless quality of untouched natural beauty
Lighting: **Soft Overcast with Gentle Diffusion**

## 4. Structural Layout
- **Left**: Dark evergreen tree cluster creating strong vertical frame and shadow mass
- **Right**: Mirroring evergreen trees balancing the composition with symmetrical weight
- **Center**: Dominant mountain peak and stream corridor forming the primary focal axis
- **Foreground**: Grassy banks embracing the blue stream with rich green textures
- **Midground**: Meadow expanse transitioning to tree bases and mountain foothills
- **Background**: Snow-crowned mountain peak and cloudy sky establishing depth
- Strong central vertical axis from stream to mountain summit guides the eye upward
Depth style: **Atmospheric Corridor Recession**

## 5. Motion
- Stream flows in gentle S-curve from mountain base toward viewer, creating dynamic depth
- Winding water path suggests continuous movement and natural flow
- Horizontal ripples and reflections indicate calm, steady current
- Clouds drift softly across the sky, implying gentle atmospheric movement
- Compositional flow draws eye from foreground stream upward along the valley to the mountain peak
- Diagonal energy from lower right to upper center creates visual momentum
Motion profile: **Meandering Stream with Upward Compositional Flow**

## 6. Technique
- **Knife work**: Angular, sharp-edged highlights on snow-capped mountain peaks and rocky surfaces
- **Fan brush**: Soft evergreen tree foliage with characteristic downward pulling strokes
- **Liner brush**: Fine details on tree trunks and delicate branches
- **Wet-on-wet blending**: Smooth sky transitions and cloud formations blended seamlessly
- **Horizontal strokes**: Water reflections with side-to-side brush movement
- **Crisscross strokes**: Grass texture using overlapping diagonal motions
- **Atmospheric blending**: Soft edges on distant mountain base creating misty depth
- **Highlight placement**: Strategic white accents on water and snow for luminosity
Signature technique: **Knife-sculpted Mountain with Wet-blended Atmospheric Haze**

## 7. Narrative Layer
"A pristine alpine sanctuary unfolds where a crystalline stream carves its ancient path through verdant meadows toward a commanding snow-crowned sentinel. Sheltering evergreens stand as silent witnesses to the water's eternal journey, while soft clouds drift overhead in quiet reverence. This is a place where time slows, where the rush of cool water and whisper of mountain wind create nature's perfect symphony of peace and solitude."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment
- Canvas prepared with **Liquid White** for smooth wet-on-wet blending throughout
- Even coverage allowing for easy sky and water manipulation
- Possibly applied more thinly in sky area for cloud definition
- Consistent moisture level enabling seamless color transitions and soft atmospheric effects
