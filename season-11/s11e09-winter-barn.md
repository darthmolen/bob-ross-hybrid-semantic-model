- **Sap Green** hints in distant evergreen forest
- **Yellow Ochre** warms the snow path with subtle earth tones

Palette identity: **Soft winter pastels**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Serene twilight stillness pervades the scene
- Cold crispness balanced by gentle warmth in sky
- Quiet solitude of rural winter life
- Peaceful coexistence of structure and nature
- Soft, diffused light suggests dawn or dusk transition
- Nostalgic simplicity of homestead life

Lighting: **Winter twilight diffusion**

## 4. Structural Layout
- **Left:** Bare trees in foreground create natural frame
- **Right:** Birch tree and open sky provide breathing space
- **Center:** Barn serves as primary focal anchor
- **Foreground:** Snow field with winding path invitation
- **Midground:** Barn structure and fence establish human scale
- **Background:** Misty evergreen forest fades into atmospheric haze

Depth style: **Layered atmospheric perspective**

- Winding snow path creates S-curve leading to barn
- Diagonal flow from lower right toward upper left

## 7. Narrative Layer
