The painting radiates stillness and peace, with misty atmospheric conditions softening the distant mountains while the lake provides a mirror of serenity.

Lighting: **Soft Diffuse Atmospheric Light** creating gentle value transitions and tranquil mood.

Depth style: **Layered Atmospheric Planes** with clear foreground, midground, and background separation enhanced by atmospheric perspective.

- **Water flow**: Still, glassy lake surface with minimal movement

## 6. Technique

- **Brush types**: Fan brush for evergreen trees and grassy textures, large brush for sky blending
- **Knife work**: Palette knife for mountain peaks creating sharp snow-capped edges, meadow grasses
- **Blending methods**: Soft circular blending for sky, horizontal strokes for atmospheric haze, criss-cross for misty transitions
- **Reflection techniques**: Pull-down reflections in the lake creating mirror effect of mountains

Signature technique: **Layered atmospheric blending** creating depth through progressive value and color temperature shifts from warm foreground to cool distant peaks.
