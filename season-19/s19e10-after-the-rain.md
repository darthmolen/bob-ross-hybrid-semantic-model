## 1. Composition

- Dramatic central waterfall cascading down rocky cliffs
- Dense forest framing both left and right sides
- Dark rocky pool in the foreground
- Misty atmospheric spray surrounding the waterfall
- No visible sky, enclosed forest setting
- Layered foliage creating depth on both sides
- Central vertical waterfall as primary focal point

A waterfall corridor composition with dense forest framing and atmospheric mist effects.

## 2. Palette

- Deep Midnight Black and Van Dyke Brown for dark rocky areas
- Prussian Blue and Phthalo Blue for water and misty waterfall
- Sap Green and Phthalo Green for lush foliage
- Titanium White for waterfall highlights and mist
- Dark Sienna for rocky textures
- Yellow Ochre for subtle warmth in highlights
- Cool overall temperature with localized warm accents

Palette identity: **Cool Misty Waterfall with Deep Forest Greens**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Fresh and rejuvenating
- Peaceful yet dynamic
- Misty and ethereal
- Cool and damp post-rain atmosphere
- Sense of natural power softened by atmospheric effects
- Contemplative and serene

Lighting: **diffuse atmospheric glow** emanating from the misty waterfall center.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left:** Dense layered evergreens and deciduous foliage
- **Right:** Mirror arrangement of forest vegetation
- **Center:** Vertical waterfall with misty spray effects
- **Foreground:** Dark rocky pool with subtle reflections
- **Midground:** Base of waterfall and surrounding rocks
- **Background:** Upper cascade and atmospheric mist

Depth style: **layered vertical depth with atmospheric fade**

## 5. Motion

- Strong vertical waterfall cascade downward
- Mist rising and diffusing from impact zone
- Implied water flow into foreground pool
- Subtle foam and spray patterns
- Compositional flow: downward cascade drawing eye to center pool
- Atmospheric movement in misty areas

Motion profile: **Vertical Waterfall Cascade with Rising Mist**

## 6. Technique

- Liquid White or Liquid Clear base for blending
- Palette knife work for rocky cliff faces and foreground stones
- Fan brush evergreens on both sides
- Vertical pull-down strokes for waterfall effect
- Feathering technique for misty atmospheric spray
- Stippling and tapping for foliage texture
- Horizontal smoothing for water pool
- Titanium White heavily loaded for waterfall highlights
- Dark underpainting for rocky areas

Signature technique: **vertical waterfall strokes with atmospheric mist blending**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"A hidden forest waterfall comes alive after a fresh rain, sending cascades of water down ancient rocks into a dark reflective pool. The mist rises and diffuses through the surrounding forest, creating an ethereal atmosphere of renewal and natural power. The enclosed composition invites the viewer into this secret sanctuary where water and forest meet in perfect harmony."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Liquid White base for smooth blending
- Possible selective dark underpainting for rocky areas
- No contact paper masking
- Wet-on-wet foundation throughout
- Pre-mixed darks for immediate depth

A foundational wet-surface approach emphasizing atmospheric blending and dramatic waterfall contrast.
