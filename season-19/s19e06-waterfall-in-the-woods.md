## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Tall rocky cliffs forming a narrow gorge
- **Water features**: Prominent vertical waterfall cascading down center, dark pool at base
- **Trees and vegetation**: Vibrant moss covering cliff faces, hanging foliage on left side
- **Sky conditions**: Not visible; enclosed canyon atmosphere
- **Structures**: None
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Vertical waterfall canyon corridor

A dramatic vertical gorge composition with a central cascading waterfall surrounded by dark rocky cliffs.

## 2. Palette

- **Primary colors used**: Midnight Black, Van Dyke Brown, Prussian Blue, Phthalo Blue, Sap Green, Phthalo Green, Titanium White
- **Warm vs cool**: Predominantly cool with deep blues and greens; minimal warm tones in rocks
- **High-contrast vs low-contrast**: Extreme high contrast between brilliant white waterfall and dark surrounding rocks
- **Signature color relationships**: Luminous white against near-black rocks, vibrant moss greens against dark stone

Palette identity: **Dramatic Dark Canyon with Brilliant White Waterfall and Vibrant Moss Accents**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Dramatic and powerful
- Cool and shadowed
- Mysterious depth within the gorge
- Energetic water movement contrasts with still, ancient rocks
- Sense of hidden, secluded sanctuary

Lighting: **diffuse sky light** creating high contrast between the illuminated waterfall and shadowed rocky gorge.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Dark rocky cliff face with bright green moss coverage, hanging vegetation
- **Right**: Textured dark cliff wall with scattered moss highlights
- **Center**: Vertical white waterfall as primary focal point
- **Foreground**: Deep blue-purple water pool with reflective surface
- **Midground**: Base of waterfall where it meets the pool
- **Background**: Upper portion of cliffs where waterfall originates

Depth style: **Vertical Tunnel Depth with Enclosed Canyon Perspective**

## 5. Motion

- Strong vertical downward waterfall flow
- Misting spray at waterfall base
- Still, reflective pool water in foreground
- Implied echo and sound within the enclosed space
- Compositional flow: **downward cascade with upward-reaching cliff walls**

Motion profile: **Vertical Cascade with Contained Energy**

## 6. Technique

- Dark toned canvas foundation
- Palette knife work for rocky cliff faces and textures
- Vertical downward strokes for waterfall using Titanium White
- Fan brush for moss application with bright green highlights
- Stippling and dabbing for foliage texture
- Horizontal blending for water pool surface
- Scraping technique for rock crevices and shadows
- Delicate highlighting for water mist and spray

Signature technique: **vertical waterfall strokes with heavy impasto against dark palette knife rocks**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"A hidden waterfall plunges through a narrow rocky gorge, its brilliant white cascade contrasting against ancient dark cliffs adorned with luminous green moss. The secluded canyon creates an intimate, powerful space where water and stone have coexisted for ages. The deep pool at the base captures the waterfall's energy in a moment of stillness before continuing its journey through the woods."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Dark toned canvas (Liquid Black or black gesso base)
- Possibly Liquid Clear in waterfall area for transparency effects
- No contact paper masking
- No textured gesso
- Dark foundation allows for dramatic light contrasts

A foundational dark-base canyon scene emphasizing extreme value contrast and vertical drama.
