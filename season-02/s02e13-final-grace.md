- **Yellow Ochre**: Ground tones, transitional foliage areas

- **High-contrast**: Brilliant white peaks against dark evergreens and deep blue sky

## 6. Technique

- Liquid White foundation for bright luminosity and smooth blending
- Palette knife work for crisp snow-capped peaks and rocky cliff faces
- Prussian Blue shadows modeling mountain dimensions
- Fan brush evergreens with varying pressure for depth and scale
- Fan brush bushes creating vibrant yellow-green foliage masses
- Pull-down reflections for mirror-like water surface
- Horizontal water strokes with subtle ripple highlights
- Cloud work with titanium white and gentle blending, dramatic formations
- Liner brush for fine tree branches and grasses
- Layered highlight technique on snow peaks for brilliance

Signature technique: **palette knife snow peaks with dimensional shadow modeling and vibrant fan brush foliage**
