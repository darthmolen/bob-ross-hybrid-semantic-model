## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Single prominent snow-capped mountain peak centered in the background with angular ridgelines and defined glacial slopes
- **Water features**: Calm reflective lake dominating the middle ground, small cascading waterfall in the lower left foreground with white water movement
- **Trees and vegetation**: Tall dark evergreens (pines) on the left side, lighter green evergreens on the right, vibrant golden-orange autumn foliage in the foreground, scattered bushes along the waterline
- **Sky conditions**: Soft peachy-pink sunset sky with subtle cloud formations and atmospheric glow
- **Structures**: None
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Mountain-lake reflection composition with waterfall accent and seasonal autumn color

A classic mirror-reflection alpine composition centered on a snow-covered peak with waterfall foreground interest.

## 2. Palette

- **Primary colors used**: Prussian Blue and Phthalo Blue for mountain shadows and water, Titanium White for snow and waterfall highlights, Cadmium Yellow and Indian Yellow for autumn foliage, Sap Green and Phthalo Green for evergreen trees, Van Dyke Brown and Burnt Umber for tree trunks and shadows, Alizarin Crimson and Bright Red for sunset sky and warm foliage accents, Yellow Ochre for ground highlights
- **Warm vs cool**: Balanced temperature with warm peachy-pink sky and golden foliage contrasting against cool blue mountain and water tones
- **High-contrast vs low-contrast**: Medium-high contrast between bright snow-covered peak and darker forested areas, with softer transitions in the reflective water
- **Signature color relationships**: Golden autumn foliage against dark evergreens, pink-tinted sky reflecting in calm blue water, bright white waterfall against shadowed rocks

Palette identity: **sunset alpine palette with golden autumn reflections**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Peaceful and contemplative
- Soft evening light quality
- Romantic and warm
- Tranquil water stillness
- Gentle atmospheric glow
- Harmonious and balanced

Lighting: **warm sunset backlight** casting a peachy glow across the sky and reflecting in the mirror-like lake surface.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Tall dark evergreen trees forming a vertical frame, cascading waterfall with white highlights, rocky foreground bank
- **Right**: Lighter green evergreens creating asymmetric balance, golden autumn foliage cluster, grassy shoreline
- **Center**: Snow-covered mountain peak as the primary focal point, perfect lake reflection doubling the visual impact
- **Foreground**: Active waterfall element with white water movement, autumn foliage in warm tones, detailed vegetation and rocks
- **Midground**: Calm lake surface with mirror reflections, base of mountain, transitional tree line
- **Background**: Mountain peak against peachy sky, soft atmospheric fade

Depth style: **mirror-reflection depth with foreground-to-background vertical symmetry**

## 5. Motion

- Cascading waterfall movement in the foreground with downward flow
- Still, glassy lake surface creating perfect reflections
- Gentle atmospheric glow suggesting light breeze
- Subtle cloud drift in the sunset sky
- Compositional flow: **vertical mirror symmetry** with waterfall providing dynamic counterpoint to the still reflective surface

## 6. Technique

- Liquid White base for smooth blending and reflective water surface
- Palette knife work for mountain peak with sharp angular strokes
- Fan brush evergreens with varying pressure for light and dark trees
- Highlight touches for autumn foliage using knife and brush dabs
- Pull-down mirror reflections for perfect water symmetry
- Waterfall created with knife-scraping and white highlight strokes
- Soft blending for peachy sunset sky
- Liner brush work for fine grasses, twigs, and waterfall details
- Horizontal smoothing strokes for calm water surface

Signature technique: **perfect pull-down reflections creating mirror symmetry between mountain and water**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"An alpine lake at sunset captures the perfect reflection of a snow-capped mountain peak, while a small waterfall adds gentle movement to the otherwise still scene. Golden autumn foliage glows in the warm evening light, creating a moment of perfect natural symmetry and tranquil beauty where the boundary between sky, mountain, and water dissolves into harmonious reflection."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Wet surface to support soft sky transitions and water reflections
- No black gesso or dark underpainting

A classic Liquid White foundation supporting luminous sunset colors and perfect mirror reflections in a balanced alpine composition.
