## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Majestic twin-peaked mountain dominating the background, dark rocky cliffs framing the waterfall
- **Water features**: Dramatic waterfall cascading in foreground, still lake in midground reflecting the mountain
- **Trees and vegetation**: Golden autumn foliage on left bank, evergreen trees on right, misty forest at lake's edge
- **Sky conditions**: Warm pink-peach sunset tones blending to soft blue, atmospheric haze
- **Structures**: None
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Waterfall-to-mountain vista with mirrored lake reflection

A dramatic waterfall foreground composition anchored by a distant mountain reflection and framed by contrasting autumn and evergreen foliage.

## 2. Palette

- **Prussian Blue and Phthalo Blue**: Mountain shadows, water depths, sky gradations
- **Titanium White**: Waterfall cascade, snow-capped peaks, water highlights, mist effects
- **Burnt Umber and Van Dyke Brown**: Dark rocky cliffs, tree trunks, earthy shadows
- **Cadmium Yellow, Indian Yellow, and Yellow Ochre**: Autumn foliage on left bank, warm highlights
- **Phthalo Green and Sap Green**: Evergreen trees, forest vegetation
- **Alizarin Crimson and Bright Red**: Sunset sky warmth, atmospheric glow
- **Warm-cool balance**: Warm sunset and autumn tones against cool blues and greens
- **High contrast**: Bright white waterfall and snow against dark rocks and shadows
- **Signature color relationships**: Pink-peach sunset reflecting on mountain snow, golden autumn against blue-green evergreens

Palette identity: **dramatic alpine palette with sunset warmth and cascading water brilliance**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Majestic and powerful
- Tranquil yet dynamic
- Serene wilderness grandeur
- Soft sunset glow
- Misty atmospheric depth
- Peaceful end-of-day calm with waterfall energy

Lighting: **backlit sunset glow** illuminating the mountain peaks and creating atmospheric haze over the lake.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Golden autumn trees, dark rocky cliff edge, waterfall origin
- **Right**: Evergreen forest, matching rocky formation
- **Center**: Cascading waterfall in foreground, still lake with mountain reflection, twin peaks rising majestically
- **Foreground**: Waterfall tumbling over dark rocks with white foam and spray
- **Midground**: Calm lake surface reflecting mountain and sky, misty forest line
- **Background**: Snow-capped mountain peaks against pink-blue sunset sky

Depth style: **layered atmospheric depth with foreground drama transitioning to distant serenity**

## 5. Motion

- Waterfall cascading powerfully downward over rocks
- Still, mirror-like lake surface in midground
- Atmospheric mist rising from forest
- Gentle vertical pull from waterfall to mountain peak
- Compositional flow: **vertical waterfall cascade with center-focused mountain reflection**
- Contrast between dynamic foreground and tranquil background

Motion profile: **cascading downward flow anchored by reflective stillness**

## 6. Technique

- Liquid White foundation for smooth blending and atmospheric effects
- Palette knife work for rocky cliffs and mountain peaks
- Pull-down strokes for waterfall cascade
- Horizontal smoothing for water highlights and foam
- Fan brush evergreens with varying pressure
- Fan brush autumn foliage with warm color highlights
- Pull-down reflections for mirror-like lake surface
- Atmospheric blending for misty forest transition
- Prussian Blue shadows on mountain for dimensional form
- Liner brush for fine branches and water details
- Titanium White loaded brush for waterfall luminosity

Signature technique: **luminous waterfall cascade with heavy white paint over dark rocky foundation**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"A powerful waterfall tumbles over dark rocks into a tranquil mountain lake as the setting sun paints the sky in soft pink and peach tones. The majestic twin-peaked mountain rises beyond the misty forest, its snow-capped summit reflected perfectly in the still waters. Autumn's golden touch on one shore contrasts with the eternal green of the evergreens, creating a harmonious balance of seasonal change and timeless wilderness beauty. The scene captures the dynamic power of falling water and the serene majesty of alpine wilderness in a single breathtaking moment."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Liquid White base for sky, mountain, and water areas
- Possible darker underpainting or heavier paint application for rocky cliff areas
- No contact paper masking
- No black gesso foundation
- Standard smooth surface preparation allowing for atmospheric blending and dramatic value contrasts

A foundational Liquid White technique with strategic darker areas supporting the dramatic interplay between luminous waterfall and shadowed rock formations.
