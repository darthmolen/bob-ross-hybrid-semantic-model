# Season 2, Episode 3 — "Ebony Sea" (1983-1984)

---

## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Gentle shoreline terrain on right side
- **Water features**: Turbulent ocean with dramatic breaking waves, dark water horizon
- **Trees and vegetation**: Dense evergreen trees in autumn foliage on right side, low bushes and grasses along waterline
- **Sky conditions**: Dramatic sunset with purple, crimson, and orange clouds, dark atmospheric masses
- **Structures**: None
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Dramatic oceanscape with autumn forest and turbulent surf

A dynamic seascape composition with crashing waves and autumn-silhouetted forest under a crimson-purple sunset sky.

---

## 2. Palette

- **Alizarin Crimson and Bright Red**: Dominant sunset sky colors creating dramatic warmth
- **Phthalo Blue and Prussian Blue**: Deep ocean water and shadow tones
- **Black Gesso**: Foundation for exceptional depth and contrast
- **Cadmium Yellow and Indian Yellow**: Sunset glow, autumn foliage highlights, and warm tree tones
- **Van Dyke Brown and Burnt Umber**: Tree trunks and dark earth accents
- **Sap Green and Phthalo Green**: Vegetation and subtle foliage undertones
- **Titanium White**: Wave foam, surf highlights, and sky luminosity
- **Warm bias**: Crimson, orange, and yellow sunset palette against cool ocean blues
- **High-contrast**: Black canvas foundation creates dramatic value separation
- **Signature color relationships**: Warm autumn oranges and sunset crimsons against dark turbulent ocean

Palette identity: **dramatic sunset oceanscape with autumn-fire palette over ebony foundation**

---

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Dramatic and powerful
- Turbulent yet majestic
- Warm sunset glow against dark waters
- Intense and moody
- Dynamic energy contrasting with silhouetted stillness
- Rare combination of autumn colors with seascape drama

Lighting: **backlit crimson sunset** creating silhouettes and illuminating wave crests.

---

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Open ocean with dramatic wave action and dark water
- **Right**: Autumn-colored evergreen forest in silhouette
- **Center**: Horizon line with sunset glow breaking through dark sky
- **Foreground**: Large breaking waves with brilliant white foam and surf
- **Midground**: Dark ocean surface with rolling swells
- **Background**: Purple and crimson sky with dark cloud masses and warm sunset glow

Depth style: **layered atmospheric depth with silhouette contrast and dynamic foreground action**

---

## 5. Motion

- Powerful ocean waves breaking toward shore
- Dynamic surf action with foam streaks
- Rolling swells and turbulent water movement
- Dark clouds suggesting atmospheric drama
- Compositional flow: **diagonal wave movement with center-focused sunset pull**
- Energetic forward motion contrasting with still forest silhouette

Motion profile: **dynamic crashing surf with powerful directional wave flow**

---

## 6. Technique

- Black gesso canvas prepared and dried
- Alizarin Crimson and purple tones applied over black foundation
- Fan brush for clouds, waves, and autumn foliage
- Horizontal and diagonal brush strokes for wave movement
- Push-up and pull-forward technique for wave foam
- Palette knife for wave highlights and dramatic surf action
- Silhouetted tree forms against luminous sky
- Liner brush for fine grasses and tree details
- Minimal blending to preserve dramatic contrast
- Layering warm autumn colors over dark base

Signature technique: **black canvas foundation with dramatic wave action and autumn-fire foliage highlights**

---

## 7. Narrative Layer

"A turbulent ebony ocean crashes against the shore as autumn trees blaze in their final glory, silhouetted against a dramatic crimson and purple sunset. The black foundation creates an intensity that amplifies both the power of the churning waves and the warm fire of the autumn forest. Nature's dual drama unfolds—the relentless energy of the sea meeting the quiet transformation of the seasonal forest—all illuminated by the sun's passionate farewell."

---

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Black gesso applied and allowed to dry completely
- Dark foundation prepared ahead of time
- Warm sunset colors applied over black base
- No Liquid White or Liquid Clear
- No contact paper masking
- No textured gesso
- Dry black foundation allows for dramatic layering and contrast

A foundational black gesso technique creating exceptional depth for a dramatic seascape with autumn elements.
