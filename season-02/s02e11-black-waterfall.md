## 2. Palette

- **Black Gesso**: Dark canvas foundation creating deep shadows and cliff faces
- **Prussian Blue and Phthalo Blue**: Dominant sky colors for stormy atmosphere
- **Alizarin Crimson**: Purple-violet tones in clouds and atmospheric effects
- **Cadmium Yellow and Indian Yellow**: Illuminated foliage on the left creating warm accent
- **Titanium White**: Waterfall highlights, cloud formations, and cabin structure
- **Van Dyke Brown and Burnt Umber**: Rocky cliff textures and earth tones
- **Sap Green and Phthalo Green**: Evergreen trees and vegetation shadows
- **Yellow Ochre**: Warm ground tones and foliage base
- **Cool bias**: Blues and purples dominate with isolated warm yellow accent
- **High-contrast**: Black foundation against bright whites and illuminated yellows
- **Signature color relationships**: Dramatic purple-blue clouds contrasting with golden-lit foliage

Palette identity: **stormy blue-purple palette with dramatic golden foliage accent and cascading white water**

## 6. Technique

- Black gesso canvas foundation for dramatic contrast
- Palette knife work for rocky cliff faces and textured formations
- Fan brush evergreens in silhouette
- Vertical pulling strokes for waterfall effect with white highlights
- Stippling and tapping for illuminated foliage texture
- Circular and sweeping strokes for cloud formations
- Cabin structure created with knife or liner brush precision
- Layering technique: light colors over dark foundation
- Atmospheric blending for cloud depth and movement

Signature technique: **dramatic waterfall highlights over dark rocky cliff with storm-lit atmospheric effects**

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Black gesso applied and dried as foundation
- Selective application of lighter tones for sky and water
- No Liquid White base (dark foundation approach)
- No contact paper masking
- No textured gesso preparation
- Dark-base technique for maximum contrast

A foundational black gesso approach emphasizing dramatic contrast between dark rock formations and luminous waterfall with storm-lit atmospheric effects.
