## 2. Palette

- **Primary colors used**: Phthalo Blue and Prussian Blue for mountains and sky, Titanium White for snow and clouds, Sap Green and Phthalo Green for evergreens, bright yellows (Cadmium Yellow, Indian Yellow) for sunlit highlights on trees and grass, Van Dyke Brown and Dark Sienna for cabin and rock shadows
- **Warm vs cool**: Balanced palette with cool blues dominating the mountains and sky, warm yellows and greens in the sunlit vegetation, earthy browns in structures
- **High-contrast vs low-contrast**: High contrast between bright snow-covered peaks and dark forest, strong value separation between light meadow and shadowed woodland
- **Signature color relationships**: Cool blue mountains against warm yellow-green highlighted evergreens, creating atmospheric depth

Palette identity: **alpine majesty palette with sun-drenched highlights**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- **Tone**: Majestic, uplifting, peaceful, expansive
- **Lighting type**: Bright direct sunlight from the right, creating strong highlights on tree foliage and grass
- **Emotional resonance**: Evokes feelings of wonder and tranquility, the grandeur of untouched wilderness, a sense of fresh mountain air and pristine natural beauty
- **Atmospheric quality**: Clear and crisp with slight misty softness at the mountain base, suggesting distance and elevation

Lighting: **bright directional sunlight** illuminating the evergreens and meadow with golden highlights.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Small cabin structure, portion of dark forest, left mountain peak
- **Right**: Two prominent tall evergreen trees with bright yellow-green highlights, extending forest line, right-side mountain formations
- **Center**: Gap between the two main evergreens revealing the mountain range, visual pathway from foreground rocks through the tree gap to distant peaks
- **Foreground**: Rocky stream bed with scattered stones, lush green grass on both sides
- **Midground**: Dense evergreen forest forming a horizontal band, cabin nestled among trees, two hero evergreens rising above the forest line
- **Background**: Snow-capped blue mountain range with atmospheric perspective, soft sky with clouds

Depth style: **layered horizontal planes with strong atmospheric perspective**

## 5. Motion

- **Water flow**: Subtle suggestion of water movement through the rocky creek bed in the foreground
- **Cloud drift**: Gentle horizontal cloud formations suggesting light breeze
- **Compositional flow lines**: Eye travels from foreground rocks upward through the gap between evergreens to the mountain peaks, strong vertical pull from the tall trees
- **Atmospheric motion**: Slight misty quality at the base of mountains suggests morning fog or atmospheric moisture
- **Visual rhythm**: Repeating vertical elements of evergreen trees create upward movement

Motion profile: **vertical ascent with gentle horizontal flow**

## 6. Technique

- **Knife work**: Mountains created with palette knife using bold angular strokes, snow highlights applied with knife edge, cabin structure built with knife
- **Brush types**: Fan brush for evergreen foliage and forest treeline, liner brush for fine tree branches and twigs, script liner for grass and small details
- **Blending methods**: Crisscross blending in sky, gentle vertical pulls for mountain shadows creating blue-purple depth
- **Highlighting**: Heavy bright highlights on evergreen foliage using yellows and yellow-greens, applied with fan brush corner tapping
- **Special effects**: Atmospheric haze at mountain base created with soft blending, rock texture created with dark and light value contrast
- **Ground treatment**: Grassy meadow created with upward liner brush strokes, varied greens and yellows for sunlit effect

Signature technique: **dramatic sunlit highlights on evergreen foliage contrasting with deep forest shadows**
