## 6. Technique

- Liquid White or Liquid Clear base for the luminous background effect
- Heavy Phthalo Blue and Prussian Blue blended for the ethereal glow
- Fan brush evergreens with detailed needle texture on the right foreground tree
- Vertical pull-down strokes for dark forest silhouettes on the left
- Knife work for rocks and stones along the riverbanks
- Pull-down reflections in the water with horizontal smoothing
- Highlight touches with Titanium White on water and grassy areas
- Liner brush for delicate grasses, twigs, and moss details
- Criss-cross blending in the background for the soft glowing effect

Signature technique: **intense blue-white atmospheric glow blended into dark forest silhouettes**
