## 1. Composition
- Dramatic waterfall cascading down the center, creating the primary focal point
- Steep valley walls flanking both sides, covered in lush grassy slopes
- Distant mountain range shrouded in dark, stormy atmosphere
- Dense evergreen forest crowning the ridgelines on both sides
- Rocky stream in the foreground with white-capped water flowing toward the viewer
- Verdant hillsides with bright highlighting suggesting soft, diffused illumination
- No structures present; pure wilderness composition

Compositional archetype: **Vertical cascade through mountain valley**

## 2. Palette
- Deep Prussian Blue and Phthalo Blue dominate the waterfall and sky
- Vibrant Sap Green creates the luminous grassy slopes
- Midnight Black anchors the stormy sky and shadows
- Titanium White provides highlights on cascading water and stream rapids
- Dark Sienna and Van Dyke Brown define the rocky elements
- Phthalo Green mixed with blacks creates the distant evergreen masses
- Yellow Ochre warms the highlighted grass areas
- Palette identity: **Storm-lit verdant valley**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Dramatic and powerful mood driven by the cascading waterfall
- Dark, brooding sky suggests an approaching or passing storm
- Lush greenery contrasts with the moody atmospheric conditions
- Sense of pristine wilderness untouched by human presence
- Both energetic and serene; the waterfall's power within a peaceful valley
- Slightly mysterious quality from the shadowed background mountains
- Lighting: **Diffused overcast** with soft illumination on the grassy slopes creating vivid green highlights against dark surroundings.

## 4. Structural Layout
- Left: Steep grassy slope with evergreen trees along the ridge
- Right: Mirroring slope with similar vegetation pattern
- Center: Waterfall descending vertically through the composition
- Foreground: Rocky stream with flowing water and white rapids
- Midground: The main waterfall drop with vertical cascades
- Background: Dark mountain silhouettes beneath stormy sky
- Depth style: **Layered vertical descent**

## 5. Motion
- Strong vertical motion of the waterfall creating downward flow
- Horizontal stream movement in the foreground drawing the eye into the scene
- Cascading water suggests continuous powerful movement
- Implied wind movement in the stormy sky
- Rapids and whitewater indicate turbulent flow over rocks
- Motion profile: **Vertical cascade with forward stream flow**

## 6. Technique
- Fan brush creates the smooth, flowing waterfall effect with vertical strokes
- Palette knife work highlights the grassy slopes with upward strokes
- Wet-on-wet blending in the dark sky for atmospheric depth
- Liner brush details the evergreen trees along the ridgelines
- Knife technique creates texture in the foreground rocks
- Phthalo Blue pulled down with fan brush for water transparency
- Highlighting technique on grass using knife-edge application
- Signature technique: **Vertical fan brush waterfall with knife-highlighted slopes**

## 7. Narrative Layer
"Deep within an untouched mountain valley, a powerful waterfall descends from storm-darkened heights, its waters catching what little light penetrates the brooding sky. The vibrant green slopes stand as testament to the life-giving force of these waters, their luminous quality suggesting brief sunlight breaking through heavy clouds. This is a place where nature's raw power and serene beauty coexist, where the thunder of falling water echoes off ancient stone, and where the verdant earth drinks deeply from the mountain's gift."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment
- Dark base coat applied across the entire canvas for the moody foundation
- Prussian Blue and black mixture blended wet into the upper portions for the stormy sky
- Darker values in the center channel where the waterfall will descend
- Wet surface maintained for blending the atmospheric background mountains
- The dark foundation allows the bright greens to pop with dramatic contrast.
