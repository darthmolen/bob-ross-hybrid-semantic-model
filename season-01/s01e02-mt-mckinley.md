## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Massive snow-capped mountain (Mt. McKinley/Denali) dominating the center-background, smaller mountain peaks to the left
- **Water features**: Still lake in the foreground reflecting the mountain and sky
- **Trees and vegetation**: Dark evergreen trees framing both sides, dense deciduous bushes and foliage along the shoreline in yellow-green and autumn tones
- **Sky conditions**: Clear blue sky with scattered white puffy clouds
- **Structures**: None
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Majestic mountain reflection with lakeside framing

A classic mountain lake reflection composition with the snow-capped peak as the dominant focal point.

## 2. Palette

- **Titanium White**: Snow-capped mountain peaks, clouds, water highlights
- **Prussian Blue**: Mountain shadows, sky gradations, water reflections
- **Phthalo Green and Sap Green**: Evergreen trees and shoreline vegetation
- **Cadmium Yellow**: Autumn bushes, foliage highlights, luminous undergrowth
- **Van Dyke Brown**: Tree trunks, earth tones, dark accents
- **Alizarin Crimson and Bright Red**: Possible touches in bushes and highlights
- **Warm and cool balance**: Cool mountain blues against warm yellow-green foliage
- **Medium-high contrast**: Bright whites against dark evergreens
- **Signature color relationships**: Prussian Blue shadows on white peaks creating dimensional mountain forms

Palette identity: **bright alpine palette with luminous snow and colorful lakeside vegetation**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Majestic and awe-inspiring
- Serene and peaceful
- Fresh and clean
- Bright daytime clarity
- Pristine wilderness quality
- Uplifting and expansive

Lighting: **full daylight with direct illumination** creating crisp mountain definition and clear reflections.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Dark evergreen trees in foreground, smaller mountain peaks in background, yellow-green bushes along shore
- **Right**: Matching evergreen trees and dense colorful bushes
- **Center**: Mt. McKinley rising majestically, reflected in the still lake below
- **Foreground**: Lake surface with reflections, vibrant shoreline vegetation
- **Midground**: Tree line and bushes transitioning to water's edge
- **Background**: Snow-capped mountain range against blue sky with clouds

Depth style: **atmospheric perspective with clear foreground-to-background progression**

## 5. Motion

- Perfectly still water surface
- Subtle cloud drift suggesting gentle atmospheric movement
- Pull-down reflections creating mirror-like quality
- No wind indicated in vegetation
- Compositional flow: Vertical emphasis with **center-focused mountain pull**
- Peaceful stasis capturing alpine tranquility

Motion profile: **serene stillness with vertical majesty**

## 6. Technique

- Liquid White foundation for smooth blending and bright luminosity
- Palette knife work for snow-capped mountain peaks and ridges
- Fan brush evergreens with varying pressure for depth
- Fan brush bushes and foliage with highlight touches
- Pull-down reflections for mirror-like water surface
- Horizontal strokes for water base
- Cloud work with titanium white and gentle blending
- Prussian Blue shadows on mountain for dimensional form
- Liner brush for fine branches and grasses

Signature technique: **palette knife mountain peaks with crisp snow definition and shadow modeling**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"The majestic Mt. McKinley rises in pristine grandeur above a tranquil alpine lake, its snow-capped peaks reflected perfectly in the still waters below. Dark evergreens frame the scene while vibrant autumn bushes line the shore, creating a celebration of Alaska's untamed wilderness. The bright, clear atmosphere captures the awe-inspiring scale and beauty of North America's highest peak in a moment of perfect calm."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Liquid White applied across the entire canvas for smooth wet-on-wet technique
- No black gesso or dark foundation
- No contact paper masking
- No textured gesso
- Bright foundation to support luminous sky, snow, and water reflections

A foundational Liquid White technique creating a bright, blendable surface ideal for crisp mountain definition and luminous atmospheric effects.
