## 1. Composition

- Majestic mountain range in the background with snow-capped peaks
- Calm lake in the foreground reflecting mountains and sky
- Evergreen trees on both sides framing the scene
- Dense forest along the shoreline
- Clouds in the sky above the mountains
- No structures
- Classic Ross archetype: mountain and water symmetry

A serene mountain-and-lake reflection composition with evergreen forest framing.

## 2. Palette

- Prussian Blue for sky and water base
- Van Dyke Brown for mountain structure and tree trunks
- Titanium White for snow caps, clouds, and water highlights
- Sap Green and Phthalo Green for evergreen foliage
- Cadmium Yellow for warmth in highlights and atmospheric glow
- Alizarin Crimson and Bright Red for subtle warmth in sky or distant mountain haze
- Cool dominant palette with warm accent highlights
- High-contrast value range from dark foreground to bright peaks

Palette identity: **cool mountain palette with crisp white highlights and evergreen depth**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Peaceful
- Serene
- Majestic
- Clear and crisp
- Inviting stillness
- Sense of grandeur and natural beauty
- Contemplative tranquility

Lighting: **diffuse natural daylight** with bright highlights on snow and water.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left:** Dense evergreen trees along the shoreline
- **Right:** Matching evergreen forest mass
- **Center:** Mountain peaks reflected in calm lake water
- **Foreground:** Lake surface with reflections and subtle ripples
- **Midground:** Tree line and lake shore
- **Background:** Towering mountain range with snow-covered peaks

Depth style: **layered planes with mirror reflection symmetry**

## 5. Motion

- Stillness of calm lake water
- Minimal ripples on water surface
- Gentle cloud drift suggestion
- Vertical mountain stability
- Compositional flow: **vertical balance with horizontal water plane**

Motion profile: **tranquil stillness with subtle atmospheric presence**

## 6. Technique

- Liquid White base for smooth blending
- Palette knife mountains with angular peaks
- Fan brush evergreens using upward strokes
- Pull-down reflections for mountain and tree mirror images
- Horizontal blending strokes for water surface
- Knife-edge snow highlights on mountain peaks
- Liner brush for tree details and fine branches
- Criss-cross blending for sky

Signature technique: **sharp palette knife mountains with soft pull-down reflections**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"A pristine mountain lake nestled in an evergreen wilderness, where towering peaks meet their perfect reflection in still waters. The scene captures the timeless majesty of untouched nature, inviting quiet contemplation and peaceful solitude."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Liquid White applied evenly across entire canvas
- No dark underpainting
- No black gesso
- No contact paper masking
- No textured gesso
- Traditional wet-on-wet foundation

A classic Liquid White foundation enabling smooth blending and soft atmospheric transitions throughout the composition.
