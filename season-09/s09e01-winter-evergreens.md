# Season 9, Episode 1 — "Winter Evergreens" (1985)

## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Snow-covered mountain peaks with purple-lavender shadows in the background
- **Water features**: Frozen lake or snow-covered ground in foreground with icy blue reflections
- **Trees and vegetation**: Tall evergreen trees (spruce/pine) framing both left and right sides, snow-laden branches, sparse background evergreens on distant peaks
- **Sky conditions**: Winter sunset with warm yellow-orange glow blending into cool purple-blue atmospheric haze
- **Structures**: None
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Winter evergreen gateway with mountain backdrop

A classic **framed mountain vista** composition with symmetrical evergreen trees creating natural borders around a winter mountain scene.

## 2. Palette

- **Primary colors used**: Prussian Blue, Phthalo Blue, Titanium White, Alizarin Crimson, Cadmium Yellow, Yellow Ochre, Van Dyke Brown, Dark Sienna, Sap Green
- **Warm vs cool**: Predominantly cool—blues and purples dominate with warm yellow-orange sunset accent
- **High-contrast vs low-contrast**: High contrast between dark evergreen silhouettes and bright snow highlights
- **Signature color relationships**: Purple-blue mountains against warm sunset sky, crisp white snow on dark tree branches, cool blue shadows in foreground snow

Palette identity: **winter sunset palette with cool purple-blue dominance and warm atmospheric glow**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Cold and crisp winter stillness
- Serene and tranquil
- Quiet solitude of a frozen landscape
- Gentle warmth from distant sunset
- Ethereal quality from purple mountain haze
- Peaceful isolation

Lighting: **winter sunset glow** creating warm sky tones contrasting with cool shadowed snow and atmospheric purple mountains.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Cluster of tall evergreen trees with snow-covered branches, dark trunks creating strong vertical lines
- **Right**: Mirroring evergreen grouping with snow highlights, balanced tree mass
- **Center**: Snow-covered mountain peaks with purple-lavender atmospheric perspective, small distant evergreens
- **Foreground**: Frozen lake or snow field with icy blue-white surface and cool shadows
- **Midground**: Open snowy expanse leading to mountain base
- **Background**: Sunset sky with yellow-orange warmth fading to purple-blue overhead

Depth style: **atmospheric perspective with purple mountain haze and strong foreground-background contrast**

## 5. Motion

- Complete stillness and frozen silence
- No wind or movement suggested
- Static, snow-laden branches
- Calm winter atmosphere
- Eye flows from dark foreground trees through open center to distant peaks
- Vertical emphasis from tall evergreens anchors the scene

Motion profile: **frozen stillness—winter tranquility with vertical tree emphasis**

## 6. Technique

- Black Gesso or dark base for dramatic contrast
- Prussian Blue and Phthalo Blue blended for sky and shadows
- Titanium White with touch of Cadmium Yellow for warm sunset glow
- Fan brush for evergreen foliage with upward strokes
- Palette knife for thick snow highlights on branches
- Alizarin Crimson mixed with blues for purple mountain shadows
- Horizontal blending for atmospheric sky transitions
- Heavy white impasto for foreground snow texture
- Liner brush for fine tree branches and details
- Purple-blue wash for distant mountain atmosphere

Signature technique: **fan brush evergreens with heavy palette knife snow highlights creating stark winter contrast**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"In the quiet depth of winter, evergreen sentinels stand guard over a frozen landscape, their snow-laden branches catching the last warm light of a distant sunset. Purple mountains rise through the cold haze, while the world below rests in frozen silence—a moment of perfect stillness in nature's winter slumber."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Black Gesso or dark underpainting for dramatic contrast
- Possibly thinned Prussian Blue for cool undertones
- No Liquid White (winter scenes often use dark base)
- No contact paper or masking
- Dark base allows for brilliant snow highlights to pop

A dark foundation creating maximum contrast for bright winter snow and enabling dramatic light-to-dark transitions.
