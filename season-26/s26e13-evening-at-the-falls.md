## 1. Composition
- Dominant central waterfall cascading through a vertical gorge
- Dark rocky cliffs framing both sides, creating a natural portal
- Silhouetted evergreen trees along the top rim against night sky
- Large boulders and rocks at the base of the falls
- Water pool in foreground with reflective surface
- Opening above reveals moonlit sky through forest canopy
- Vertical orientation emphasizes the dramatic height of the cascade
Compositional archetype: **Illuminated gorge waterfall**

## 2. Palette
- Deep midnight blacks and Prussian blues dominate the scene
- Brilliant whites and silvery grays for cascading water
- Cool blue-gray tones for moonlit highlights on rocks
- Monochromatic blue scheme creates nocturnal unity
- Subtle gradations from deep shadow to moonlit surfaces
- Minimal warm tones, maintaining cold night atmosphere
Palette identity: **Midnight blue chiaroscuro**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Mysterious and sublime nighttime drama
- Powerful, thundering energy contained within intimate space
- Moonlight creates theatrical spotlight effect on the falls
- Sense of discovery and hidden sanctuary
- Cool, crisp evening air implied
- Romantic and awe-inspiring atmosphere
Lighting: **Moonlight** illuminating the cascade through forest opening.

## 4. Structural Layout
- Center: Towering waterfall as dominant focal element
- Left/Right: Dark cliff walls forming natural frame
- Foreground: Large foreground rocks and reflective pool
- Midground: Main waterfall cascade with mist and spray
- Background: Sky opening with silhouetted tree line
- Vertical depth emphasized through layered darkness
Depth style: **Vertical chasm with moonlit focal plane**

## 5. Motion
- Strong downward cascade of water over multiple tiers
- Turbulent churning at the base where water meets pool
- Misty spray rising from impact zone
- Stillness in foreground pool contrasts with falling water
- Implied rush of sound and power
Motion profile: **Thundering vertical cascade**

## 6. Technique
- Heavy dark base establishes nocturnal foundation
- Highlight technique with pure whites for luminous water
- Knife work for sharp rock edges and water texture
- Silhouette technique for trees against lighter sky
- Push-and-pull method for water volume and dimension
- Careful value control to maintain moonlit drama
Signature technique: **Moonlit highlight glazing on cascading water**

## 7. Narrative Layer
"Deep in the forest gorge, where few travelers venture, a waterfall thunders through the darkness, revealed only when the moon breaks through the canopy above. The silvery cascade becomes a beacon in the night, transforming the hidden chasm into a cathedral of natural power and celestial light. This is a place where the earth's raw energy meets the gentle touch of moonbeams, creating a sanctuary both wild and sacred."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment
The canvas was prepared with a heavy application of Liquid Black or a dark blue-black mixture, creating the deep nocturnal foundation essential for this nighttime scene. This dark base allowed the moonlit highlights to appear luminous and dramatic when applied later, with the wet-on-wet technique enabling smooth transitions between the deep shadows and silvery illuminated areas. The preparation established the mysterious, theatrical atmosphere from the very beginning.
