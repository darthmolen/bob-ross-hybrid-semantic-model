## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment
The canvas was treated with a thin, even layer of Liquid White, creating a wet surface that enabled smooth blending of the atmospheric mist and soft sky gradations. This preparation allowed the characteristic wet-on-wet technique to achieve seamless transitions between the glowing yellow-cream atmospheric effects and the soft background elements, while maintaining distinct separation between the sharp foreground tree and the misty background layers.
