## 1. Composition
- Snow-covered mountain peak dominating the left-center background
- Aurora borealis streaming in sweeping curtains across the night sky
- Small cabin with snow-laden roof nestled in the foreground
- Evergreen trees heavily laden with snow flanking both sides
- Dark lake or frozen water body in the midground reflecting the sky
- Starfield scattered across the deep blue-black heavens
This is a winter nocturne composition anchored by human shelter beneath celestial phenomenon.

## 2. Palette
- Deep midnight blues and purples forming the night sky base
- Luminous cyan and bright white for the aurora borealis bands
- Pure white and light blue highlights on snow surfaces
- Dark indigo and black for shadows and depth
- Subtle warm amber glow from cabin window
- Touches of lavender in transitional aurora zones
Palette identity: **Arctic night luminescence**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Serene and otherworldly, capturing the magic of polar regions
- Profound stillness punctuated by celestial motion
- Sense of solitude and wonder beneath nature's grandest light show
- Cold, crisp clarity of a frozen winter night
- Peaceful coexistence of human presence and wilderness
Lighting: **Aurora-lit nocturne** with ethereal bioluminescent quality casting subtle reflections across snow and water.

## 4. Structural Layout
- **Left:** Snow-heavy evergreens framing the edge; mountain slope rising toward the peak
- **Right:** Dense cluster of frosted conifers creating dark-light contrast
- **Center:** Cabin positioned as focal anchor; mountain peak aligned above
- **Foreground:** Snow-covered ground with cabin and foundation trees establishing human scale
- **Midground:** Dark water body creating reflective surface and spatial separation
- **Background:** Towering snow-clad mountain and expansive night sky with aurora display

Depth style: **Layered nocturnal recession**

## 5. Motion
- Aurora borealis flows in dramatic sweeping curves from left to upper right
- Ribbon-like waves suggest continuous celestial movement
- Vertical curtains of light create downward streaming effect
- Otherwise motionless landscape emphasizes the aurora's dynamic energy
- Implied breeze suggested by the direction of light streams
Motion profile: **Ethereal sky flow**

## 6. Technique
- Palette knife work creating thick, textured snow on trees and mountain
- Fan brush stippling for snow-laden evergreen foliage
- Smooth blending for aurora's luminous, flowing bands
- Circular or sweeping strokes to create aurora's wave patterns
- Dark base canvas preserved for night sky depth
- Highlight touches using liquid white or bright titanium white
- Dabbing or flicking motion for scattered starfield
- Wet-on-wet technique allowing aurora colors to blend seamlessly
Signature technique: **Blended aurora effects over preserved dark canvas**

## 7. Narrative Layer
"A lone cabin stands sentinel in the frozen wilderness, its warm glow a tiny beacon beneath the heavens' greatest spectacle. The aurora borealis unfurls across the night in ribbons of celestial light, transforming the snow-clad landscape into a dreamscape where earth meets cosmos. In this remote sanctuary, the mountain watches eternally as nature's most magical phenomenon dances overhead, a reminder that even in the deepest winter darkness, beauty illuminates the world."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment
- Black or very dark blue base applied across entire canvas
- Dark foundation preserved strategically for night sky depth
- Background darkness allowed to show through between aurora bands
- Foundation provides natural shadows and nocturnal atmosphere without additional work
The canvas began in darkness, creating the perfect void for celestial light to emerge.
