## 1. Composition
- Massive snow-covered mountain range dominates the background with prominent central peak
- Dark foothills create layered depth between foreground and alpine peaks
- Calm reflective lake or river occupies the lower third
- Tall deciduous trees (birch-like) frame the left foreground
- Single evergreen and bushy vegetation anchor the right foreground
- Pink-lavender sky with soft cloud formations

A classic alpine panorama with strong vertical mountains balanced by horizontal water and framing vegetation.

## 2. Palette
- **Titanium White** and **Prussian Blue** for snow-covered peaks and highlights
- **Pink/Lavender** tones for atmospheric sky treatment
- **Midnight Black** and **Dark Sienna** for foothills and shadows
- **Sap Green** and **Yellow Ochre** for spring/summer vegetation
- **Phthalo Blue** and cool grays for water reflections
- Warm greens and yellows for foreground bushes

Palette identity: **Soft alpine pastels with dramatic dark contrasts**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Serene and contemplative with pristine wilderness quality
- Dreamy, ethereal atmosphere created by the pink-tinted sky
- Complete stillness conveyed through mirror-calm water
- Majestic grandeur balanced with intimate tranquility
- Sense of untouched, remote mountain solitude

Lighting: **Soft diffused dawn or dusk light** casting pink atmospheric glow across the alpine scene

## 4. Structural Layout
- **Left:** Tall slender deciduous trees with light-colored trunks rising from waterline
- **Right:** Dark evergreen tree at edge with rounded bushy vegetation below
- **Center:** Dominant snow-covered mountain range with sharp central peak
- **Foreground:** Still water with perfect reflections and low vegetation
- **Midground:** Dark mountain foothills creating horizontal band of depth
- **Background:** Dramatic alpine peaks with snow patterns and pink sky

Depth style: **Multi-plane layering with strong atmospheric perspective**

## 5. Motion
- Absolute stillness dominates the scene
- Water surface perfectly calm, creating mirror reflections
- No wind movement in trees or sky
- Static snow patterns on stationary peaks
- Sense of frozen moment in time

Motion profile: **Complete tranquility and stillness**

## 6. Technique
- **Palette knife work** for mountain peaks, snow highlights, and rocky structures
- **Fan brush** for evergreen foliage and delicate tree branches
- **Wet-on-wet blending** for sky gradients and atmospheric effects
- **Vertical pull-down technique** for water reflections
- **Highlight placement** with pure white for snow caps and water shimmer
- **Layered mountain building** from dark base to light peaks
- **Stippling or pushing motion** for bushy foreground vegetation

Signature technique: **Dramatic knife-work mountain construction with soft atmospheric blending**

## 7. Narrative Layer
"A remote alpine sanctuary rises from perfectly still waters, where spring vegetation meets eternal snow. The pink-washed sky suggests the liminal hour of dawn or dusk, when the mountains stand in silent witness to the changing light. This is a place untouched by time, where nature's grandeur and delicate beauty exist in perfect harmony."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment
- **Gradient wash** of pink and lavender applied to upper canvas for sky foundation
- **Liquid white or clear base** on lower canvas for wet-on-wet water technique
- **Possible dark undertone** in water area to enhance depth and reflection contrast
- Wet canvas throughout to allow smooth blending and reflection techniques

Foundation emphasizes soft atmospheric quality with preparation for both mountain drama and water serenity.
