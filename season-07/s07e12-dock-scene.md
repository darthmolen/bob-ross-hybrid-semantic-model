Prussian blue and white dominate the sky with touches of phthalo blue for depth. Soft gray-lavender mist creates atmospheric perspective in the background. The evergreen tree introduces sap green and van dyke brown accents. Titanium white highlights the cloud formations and adds dimension to the wood planking. Dark shadows use van dyke brown and prussian blue mixtures.

The painting evokes nostalgia and quiet abandonment, with weathered structures suggesting maritime history and the passage of time. The misty background creates a dreamlike quality, softening the harsh reality of aging buildings. There's a peaceful solitude to the scene, as if capturing an early morning moment when fog still clings to the water. The lone evergreen adds a touch of resilience and life amid the decay. **Soft diffused morning light through coastal mist**
