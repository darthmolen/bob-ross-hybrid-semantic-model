# Season 6, Episode 8 — "Horizons West" (1986)

## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Dramatic snow-capped mountain with jagged peaks, warm-toned foothills
- **Water features**: Tranquil lake with mirror-like reflections
- **Trees and vegetation**: Dark evergreen trees framing both sides, lighter deciduous trees on right
- **Sky conditions**: Intense sunset sky with warm yellow, orange, and pink atmospheric glow
- **Structures**: None
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Mountain-centered sunset lake scene

A classic western mountain reflection composition with dramatic sunset lighting and symmetrical tree framing.

## 2. Palette

- **Primary colors used**: Alizarin Crimson, Bright Red, Cadmium Yellow, Indian Yellow, Titanium White, Van Dyke Brown, Midnight Black, Phthalo Blue
- **Warm vs cool**: Heavily warm-biased with intense sunset oranges, reds, and yellows; cool blues and purples in water reflections
- **High-contrast vs low-contrast**: High contrast between bright sky and dark tree silhouettes; dramatic value range
- **Signature color relationships**: Warm yellows and oranges against cool purple-blue water reflections; white mountain peaks against warm sky

Palette identity: **vibrant sunset palette with warm oranges and dramatic cool-warm contrasts**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- **Tone**: Dramatic, energetic, awe-inspiring, warm
- **Lighting type**: Intense sunset glow with radiant atmospheric light
- **Emotional resonance**: Sense of grandeur and natural majesty; vibrant western wilderness

Lighting: **radiant sunset glow** bathing the mountain and sky in warm, luminous colors.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Cluster of dark evergreen trees forming strong vertical silhouettes
- **Right**: Mixed evergreens and lighter deciduous trees creating varied texture
- **Center**: Prominent snow-capped mountain peak with lake reflections below
- **Foreground**: Warm reddish-orange banks with subtle vegetation texture
- **Midground**: Reflective lake surface with purple-blue tones
- **Background**: Glowing sunset sky transitioning from yellow to pink-orange

Depth style: **layered horizontal planes with atmospheric warm glow**

## 5. Motion

- **Water flow**: Still, calm lake surface
- **Wave direction**: No wave action; mirror-like reflections
- **Cloud drift**: Diffuse atmospheric glow without defined cloud movement
- **Compositional flow lines**: Horizontal water line leading to vertical mountain; trees frame inward toward center
- **Atmospheric motion**: Radiant light emanating from sky horizon

Motion profile: **静止 reflection with central mountain focus and radiant atmospheric glow**

## 6. Technique

- **Brush types**: Fan brush for evergreen trees and foliage; liner brush for fine tree details
- **Knife work**: Palette knife for mountain peaks and snow highlights; knife-edge banks
- **Blending methods**: Wet-on-wet blending for sky gradients; criss-cross for atmospheric transitions
- **Reflection techniques**: Pull-down mirror reflections in water; horizontal smoothing for lake surface
- **Special effects**: Luminous glow in sky; warm undertones throughout; dark tree silhouettes for contrast

Signature technique: **palette knife mountain work with vibrant wet-on-wet sunset blending and mirror reflections**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"A majestic snow-capped mountain rises above a tranquil lake under the brilliant glow of a western sunset. The warm, fiery sky casts radiant colors across the landscape while dark evergreens stand as silent sentinels, framing this moment of natural splendor. The still water mirrors the mountain's grandeur, creating perfect symmetry in this serene wilderness scene."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- **Liquid White**: Applied for wet-on-wet blending in sky and water areas
- **Warm undertones**: Pre-mixed warm yellows and oranges for sunset base
- **No black gesso**: Bright foundation for luminous sunset effect
- **No contact paper**: Organic edges throughout
- **No masking**: Freehand mountain and tree placement

A bright, warm-toned foundation enabling vibrant sunset colors and smooth atmospheric blending.
