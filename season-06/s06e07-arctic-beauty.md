## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Snow-covered mountain peak with shadowed slopes and ridges
- **Right**: Dense cluster of dark evergreen trees
- **Center**: Flowing river with white water cascading toward viewer
- **Foreground**: Golden meadow grasses and prominent water highlights
- **Midground**: Dark evergreen forest creating horizontal band
- **Background**: Mountain peak rising into purple-tinted sky

Depth style: **layered horizontal planes with atmospheric purple fade**

## 5. Motion

- Vigorous river flow moving forward toward viewer
- White water highlights suggesting rapids and current
- Cascading water effect down from mountain
- Gentle cloud drift in upper atmosphere
- Compositional flow: **downward mountain-to-meadow cascade with forward water thrust**

---

## 6. Technique

- Palette knife for mountain structure and snow coverage
- Heavy knife texture for snow ridges and slopes
- Fan brush for evergreen trees
- Liner brush for meadow grasses and fine details
- Horizontal blending strokes for water surface
- White highlight strokes for water movement and foam
- Vertical pulling motions for water flow
- Push-up technique for misty water spray

Signature technique: **dynamic white water highlights over blue-toned river base with heavy knife-textured mountain**
