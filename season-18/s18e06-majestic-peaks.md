## 1. Composition

- Prominent snow-covered mountain range with multiple dramatic peaks
- Calm alpine lake in the foreground reflecting the mountains
- Single tall evergreen tree on the right side framing the scene
- Bright yellow-green bushes in the immediate foreground
- Misty atmospheric zone between water and mountain base
- Clear blue sky with soft white clouds
- No structures or human elements

A classic **Mountain Lake Reflection** composition with dramatic snow-capped peaks and vibrant foreground vegetation.

## 2. Palette

- Bright Phthalo Blue for sky and water base
- Titanium White for snow coverage and cloud highlights
- Prussian Blue and Van Dyke Brown for mountain shadows and crevices
- Sap Green and Phthalo Green for the evergreen tree
- Cadmium Yellow and Yellow Ochre for luminous foreground bushes
- Soft grays and lavenders for atmospheric mist
- Cool to neutral temperature with warm accent in foreground

**Palette identity: "Crisp Alpine Palette with Luminous Spring Foliage"**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Serene and majestic
- Clear mountain air quality
- Bright and uplifting
- Peaceful and pristine
- Fresh spring or early summer feeling
- Sense of vastness and grandeur

**Lighting: "Direct Overhead Illumination"** creating sharp snow highlights and clear reflections.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left:** Mountain peaks with snow and shadow detail
- **Right:** Tall evergreen tree framing the composition
- **Center:** Lake surface with mountain reflection
- **Foreground:** Bright yellow-green bushes creating visual anchor
- **Midground:** Calm water surface and misty atmospheric zone
- **Background:** Snow-covered mountain range and blue sky

**Depth style: "Layered Atmospheric Planes"** with distinct foreground, mist zone, and mountain backdrop.

## 5. Motion

- Still, mirror-like water surface
- Subtle atmospheric haze suggesting gentle air movement
- Minimal cloud drift in upper sky
- Vertical evergreen creates upward visual pull
- Overall sense of calm stillness

**Motion profile: "Alpine Stillness with Reflective Calm"**

## 6. Technique

- Liquid White base for smooth blending
- Palette knife work for mountain peaks and snow highlights
- Pull-down reflection technique for water
- Fan brush for evergreen tree structure
- Bright highlight technique for snow-covered slopes
- Soft blending for atmospheric mist zone
- Bold color application for foreground bushes
- Horizontal smoothing strokes for water surface

**Signature technique: "Dramatic palette knife mountains with mirror-perfect water reflections"**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"A pristine alpine lake nestled beneath towering snow-covered peaks, where crystal-clear water reflects the majesty of the mountains above. Vibrant spring foliage bursts forth in the foreground, while a solitary evergreen stands sentinel, framing this peaceful sanctuary of natural grandeur."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment

- Liquid White applied across entire canvas
- Smooth wet surface for blending sky and water
- No dark underpainting
- No contact paper masking
- No textured gesso

A bright foundation approach emphasizing luminosity and smooth color transitions for a classic mountain reflection scene.
