- **High contrast**: Dramatic value range from deep darks to brilliant light
- **Cool-to-warm transition**: Purple atmosphere shifting to warm yellow highlights
- **Ethereal color relationships**: Purple glow contrasting with yellow-green foliage

Palette identity: **Mystical Purple Glow with Luminous Yellow-Green Foliage**

- **Magical and enchanted**: Otherworldly luminous quality
- **Mysterious**: Glowing portal-like center light
- **Serene yet fantastical**: Peaceful but supernatural atmosphere
- **Ethereal**: Dreamlike color palette and soft atmospheric blending
- **Inviting**: Path draws viewer into the glowing center
- **Transcendent**: Spiritual, fairy-tale quality

Depth style: **Tunnel Perspective with Radiant Central Vanishing Point**

- **Atmospheric radiation**: Light emanating outward from center

Motion profile: **Radial Light Emanation with Strong Inward Perspective Pull**

## 6. Technique

- **Dark toned foundation**: Black base for dramatic contrast
- **Fan brush foliage**: Layered bushes and tree masses

- **Heavy highlighting**: Bright yellow-green edges on foliage
- **Atmospheric blending**: Soft purple-pink glow achieved through gentle brush work
- **Criss-cross blending**: Merging purple tones in background
- **Color layering**: Multiple passes to build luminous depth
