## 6. Technique

- Liquid White or Liquid Clear base for extensive wet-on-wet blending
- Heavy blending and feathering for atmospheric mist effects

- Circular and cross-hatching blending for the golden glow
- Atmospheric perspective through progressive lightening
- Pull-down reflections in water
- Horizontal smoothing strokes for mist layers
- Titanium White loaded heavily for brightest glow areas

**Signature technique: "Layered atmospheric blending creating luminous golden mist"**
