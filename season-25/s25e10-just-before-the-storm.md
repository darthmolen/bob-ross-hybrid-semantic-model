# Season 25, Episode 10 — "Just Before the Storm" (1992)

## 1. Composition

- **Seascape**: Turbulent tropical ocean scene
- **Palm tree**: Large silhouetted palm tree on the left foreground
- **Waves**: Multiple rolling waves with white foam and breaking crests
- **Distant land**: Sandy shoreline or landmass visible on the right
- **Storm clouds**: Dramatic, dark storm clouds dominating the upper portion
- **Sky conditions**: Heavy, threatening clouds with minimal light breaking through
- **No structures**: Pure natural scene
- **Overall compositional archetype**: Tropical storm seascape

A dramatic tropical seascape composition with stormy clouds and crashing waves framed by a silhouetted palm tree.

## 2. Palette

- **Midnight Black and Prussian Blue**: Deep, dark tones for storm clouds and turbulent water
- **Titanium White**: Brilliant highlights for wave crests, foam, and cloud edges
- **Van Dyke Brown and Dark Sienna**: Palm tree trunk and branches
- **Sap Green**: Palm fronds
- **Cool blues and grays**: Ocean depths and atmospheric effects
- **Alizarin Crimson**: Subtle warm undertones in clouds
- **High contrast**: Dramatic value relationships between dark storm elements and white foam

Palette identity: **Dramatic Storm Blues with Explosive White Highlights**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Dramatic and intense
- Ominous and threatening
- Powerful and turbulent
- Nature's raw energy on display
- Sense of imminent storm arrival
- Dark, heavy atmosphere with limited light penetration
- Emotional tension and anticipation

Lighting: **storm-diffused light** filtering weakly through heavy clouds with dramatic contrast.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Dominant silhouetted palm tree anchoring the foreground
- **Right**: Distant shoreline or landmass with lighter values
- **Center**: Turbulent ocean waves rolling toward the viewer
- **Foreground**: Active wave action with white foam and spray
- **Midground**: Rolling ocean swells building in intensity
- **Background**: Dark storm clouds with subtle tonal variations and distant land

Depth style: **layered atmospheric drama with foreground-to-background wave progression**

## 5. Motion

- **Ocean waves**: Strong directional movement toward the shore and viewer
- **Wave crests**: Breaking foam with white highlights
- **Water turbulence**: Churning, dynamic ocean surface
- **Cloud movement**: Heavy, brooding clouds suggesting wind and atmospheric pressure
- **Palm fronds**: Implied movement from storm winds
- **Compositional flow**: Diagonal wave progression from left to right
- **Atmospheric motion**: Storm energy permeating the entire scene

Motion profile: **turbulent ocean energy with directional wave flow and storm tension**

## 6. Technique

- **Black Gesso base**: Dark foundation for dramatic contrast
- **Palette knife**: Wave shapes, foam highlights, and water texture
- **Fan brush**: Palm fronds, cloud formations, and atmospheric blending
- **Large brush work**: Sweeping cloud masses and ocean depths
- **Heavy white highlights**: Thick titanium white for crashing foam
- **Criss-cross blending**: Storm cloud texture and atmospheric effects
- **Liner brush**: Fine details on palm tree trunk and branches
- **Layered knife work**: Multiple passes for wave dimension and foam spray

Signature technique: **dramatic knife-applied wave highlights over dark ocean base with explosive white foam**

## 7. Narrative Layer

"The calm before nature's fury—a tropical paradise moments before a storm unleashes its power. The lone palm tree stands sentinel against the darkening sky while waves build in intensity, white foam catching the last fragments of light. Heavy clouds press down, creating a tense atmosphere of anticipation and raw natural energy."

- Creates foundation for storm atmosphere
- Allows brilliant whites to pop dramatically
- No Liquid White or Liquid Clear overlay
- No contact paper masking
- Oval canvas format
