## 6. Technique

- **Canvas preparation**: Likely Liquid White for smooth blending in water and sky
- **Atmospheric blending**: Extensive soft blending for misty background layers, creating multiple receding planes
- **Tree techniques**: Palette knife work for cypress trunk texture, fan brush for hanging moss in downward strokes
- **Foliage rendering**: Fan brush tapping for moss and vegetation clusters, creating organic draping forms
- **Water technique**: Horizontal blending strokes for still water, subtle pull-down reflections from the cypress tree
- **Highlight placement**: Titanium White for water sparkles and the white bird, creating focal points
- **Depth creation**: Progressive lightening and softening of background elements, atmospheric perspective through value and edge control
- **Detail work**: Liner brush for small stumps, grasses, and the delicate white bird

Signature technique: **Hanging Spanish moss rendered with downward fan brush strokes** creating the iconic Southern swamp aesthetic.
