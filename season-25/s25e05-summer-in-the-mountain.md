## 1. Composition

- **Landforms**: Majestic snow-capped mountain range with multiple peaks, rolling foothills at base
- **Water features**: Tranquil lake in middle ground, mirror-like surface reflecting mountains and sky
- **Trees and vegetation**: Tall evergreen trees flanking both sides, dense dark bushes in foreground, golden summer meadow grasses
- **Sky conditions**: Dramatic cloud formations with white and peachy-pink highlights, blue sky visible through clouds

A classic mountain lake panorama framed by evergreens with expansive summer meadow foreground.

## 2. Palette

- Titanium White dominates snow-capped peaks and billowing clouds
- Prussian Blue creates sky and water foundation
- Alizarin Crimson and Bright Red add warm peachy-pink tones to clouds
- Sap Green forms the meadow base and evergreen foliage
- Yellow Ochre and Indian Yellow create golden summer grass highlights
- Van Dyke Brown and Dark Sienna provide depth in shadows and bushes
- Midnight Black adds dark accents in foliage
- Cadmium Yellow brings brightness to meadow highlights

Palette identity: **Bright summer alpine palette with warm meadow golds and cool mountain blues**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere

- Uplifting and serene
- Bright summer daylight
- Expansive and breathtaking
- Clear mountain air
- Peaceful and inviting
- Celebratory warmth

Lighting: **Bright overhead daylight** illuminating the meadow and mountain peaks with dramatic cloud highlights.

## 4. Structural Layout

- **Left**: Cluster of dark evergreen trees framing the left edge, golden meadow extending to water's edge
- **Right**: Tall prominent evergreen trees anchoring the right side, foreground bushes
- **Center**: Snow-capped mountain range dominating the background, lake reflecting peaks and sky
- **Foreground**: Golden summer meadow grasses with dark green bushes providing contrast
- **Midground**: Calm lake surface with subtle reflections, transition from meadow to water
- **Background**: Multiple mountain peaks with snow, dramatic cloud formations above

Depth style: **Layered planes with atmospheric clarity** from vibrant foreground to distant mountain peaks.

## 5. Motion

- Gentle cloud drift from left to right
- Still, reflective lake surface
- Implied breeze through meadow grasses
- Compositional flow: eye moves from foreground meadow upward to mountains
- Atmospheric clarity with no mist or haze

Motion profile: **Static mountain serenity with subtle cloud movement**

## 6. Technique

- Palette knife work for mountain peaks and snow definition
- Fan brush for evergreen trees with downward pulling strokes
- Criss-cross blending for cloud formations
- Horizontal smoothing strokes for lake surface
- Upward pulling strokes for meadow grasses
- Stippling and dabbing for bushes and foliage texture
- Reflection technique with muted colors in water
- Feathering for cloud edges and atmospheric blending

Signature technique: **Palette knife mountain peaks with dramatic snow highlights** contrasted against soft summer meadow.

## 7. Narrative Layer

Evergreen sentinels frame the vista, inviting the viewer into this peaceful alpine sanctuary."
