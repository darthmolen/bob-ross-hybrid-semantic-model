- **Left:** Birch tree dominates foreground with textured bark, grassy bank slopes toward water
- **Right:** Cluster of deciduous trees with yellow-green foliage, meadow extends to background, small structure visible
- **Center:** Brook winds dynamically through composition with white water highlights and reflections
- **Foreground:** Close view of bubbling brook with detailed water movement over rocks

## 6. Technique

- Fan brush strokes for meadow grass with upward directional pulls
- Palette knife work for water highlights and reflective surfaces
- Wet-on-wet blending for atmospheric background and sky
- Vertical downward strokes for tree trunks
- Highlight technique with bright yellows tapped onto tree foliage
- Horizontal knife strokes for water flow and ripples
- Dark and light value contrast for water depth and movement
- Liner brush for fine details on birch bark texture and small branches

Signature technique: **Knife-highlighted water with bubbling white caps**
