## 6. Technique

- Heavy wet-on-wet blending for atmospheric haze
- Fan brush for soft, diffused background foliage
- Palette knife for textured foreground grasses
- Liner brush for delicate bare branches
- Liquid White base allowing extensive blending
- Minimal detail work, emphasis on tonal gradation

Signature technique: **Atmospheric glazing with wet-on-wet diffusion**
