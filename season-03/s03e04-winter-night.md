## 1. Composition
- Florida state silhouette serves as compositional frame containing coastal sunset scene
- Two palm trees in silhouette positioned left of state outline against blue-gray background
- Ocean waves and beach shoreline within Florida shape
- Small structure (lighthouse or chimney) at top right edge of state outline
- Grass and vegetation at base of palm trees and within coastal scene
- Sky transitions from warm sunset colors to cooler blues
This is a **geographic-composite archetype** with dual scenes separated by state boundary.

## 2. Palette
- Periwinkle and light blue for sky outside Florida boundary
- Dark purple and coral pink in sky within Florida scene
- Bright orange and yellow in sunset horizon
- Ocean blues and seafoam greens in water
- Sandy beige and tan for shoreline
- Dark sienna and black for palm tree silhouettes
- Olive and sage greens for vegetation
Palette identity: **Tropical-twilight contrast with warm-cool division**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Dual atmospheric zones: serene tropical exterior and vibrant sunset interior
- Peaceful evening transition with contemplative quality
- Nostalgic regional identity through geographic shape
- Warm glow contained within boundary suggests enclosed warmth
- Calm water and still palms convey tranquility
Lighting: **Sunset backlighting with silhouette contrast**

## 4. Structural Layout
- **Left:** Palm tree silhouettes with grass base, occupying external space
- **Right:** Florida outline dominates right two-thirds of canvas
- **Center:** Sunset horizon line bisects Florida shape horizontally
- **Foreground:** Beach and vegetation within lower Florida region, palm grass outside
- **Midground:** Ocean waters and waves within state boundary
- **Background:** Sunset sky within Florida, cool blue sky outside
Depth style: **Framed-composite with contained atmospheric depth**

## 5. Motion
- Ocean waves suggest gentle rolling motion toward shore
- Horizontal cloud streaks indicate wind movement left to right
- Sunset radiates outward from horizon point
- Palm fronds appear still, suggesting calm evening air
- Compositional flow moves from external cool zone into warm interior scene
Motion profile: **Gentle oceanic with radial sunset energy**

## 6. Technique
- Silhouette technique for palm trees using dark loading
- Wet-on-wet blending for sunset gradient transitions
- Horizontal knife strokes for ocean wave highlights
- Soft blending for sky color transitions
- Crisp edge work defining Florida state boundary
- Fan brush apparent in palm frond details
Signature technique: **Silhouette contrast against blended atmospheric background**

## 7. Narrative Layer
"This composition presents Florida as a vessel containing the warmth of coastal sunset, while the external world maintains cooler, quieter tones. The palm trees stand as sentinels outside paradise, observing rather than participating in the vibrant scene within. The geographic boundary becomes both frame and threshold, suggesting the contained magic of place and memory. It speaks to regional identity and the way certain landscapes hold their own climate of feeling, separate from the surrounding world."

## 8. Initial Canvas Treatment
- Blue base coat applied to entire canvas for sky foundation
- Darker values loaded for boundary definition
- Gradient pre-blend likely for sunset transition zones
- Clean division maintained between interior and exterior atmospheric treatments
The foundation establishes dual atmospheric zones with distinct color temperatures separated by geographic boundary.
