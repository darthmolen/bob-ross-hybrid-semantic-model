- Winding path creates strong S-curve diagonal movement from lower right into misty center

- Eye flows along path curve, drawn deeper into foggy forest interior
- Mist creates lateral movement across composition, unifying elements

---

## 6. Technique
- Heavy wet-on-wet blending for atmospheric mist effects throughout background
- Fan brush for evergreen conifer foliage with distinctive downward stroke patterns
- Palette knife work for path highlights and textural grass elements
- Liner brush for tree trunks and delicate branch details
- Cross-hatching or stippling motion for deciduous tree foliage
- Soft blending technique to create progressive atmospheric fade from foreground to background
- Thin paint applications in background for translucent mist quality
- Heavier impasto in foreground grass for textural contrast

Signature technique: **Graduated atmospheric blending with mist saturation**
