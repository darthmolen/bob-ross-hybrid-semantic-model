- Vertical thrust of tall bare tree trunks drawing eye upward
- Diagonal flow from lower corners toward central mountain peaks
- Cloud formations create horizontal movement across purple sky
- Tree canopy masses create rhythmic texture across middle plane
- Mountain peaks establish strong upward triangular motion
- Overall compositional flow moves from warm foreground depth to cool distant heights

## 6. Technique
- **Fan brush**: Dense foliage masses created with upward tapping motions
- **Liner brush**: Fine branches and dead tree details
- **1-inch brush**: Sky gradations and cloud base layers
- **Knife work**: Mountain peaks shaped with angular strokes, snow highlights
- **Layering**: Multiple yellow-green tones built up for foliage depth
- **Highlight technique**: Bright yellow and white touches on sunlit foliage edges
- **Trunk technique**: Dark vertical strokes with lighter side highlights
- **Sky blending**: Purple and blue tones softly merged with criss-cross strokes

Signature technique: **Vibrant autumn foliage masses with fan brush tapping**

## 7. Narrative Layer
