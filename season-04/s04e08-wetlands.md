Palette identity: **autumn wetland with muted atmospheric grays**

## 3. Mood & Atmosphere
- Humid, overcast ambiance with soft diffused light
- Mysterious quality from Spanish moss and mist
- Tranquil stillness of undisturbed water
- Melancholic autumn tones suggest seasonal transition
- Enclosed, intimate feeling from overhanging vegetation
- Southern swamp character with hanging moss
Lighting: **overcast diffused with no direct sun**

- **Left:** Dark tree cluster with autumn foliage, vegetation along bank
- **Right:** Prominent Spanish moss hanging from trees, mixed green and brown foliage
- **Center:** Still water with reflections, receding into misty background
- **Foreground:** Water surface with bank vegetation in warm autumn colors
- **Background:** Hazy tree line creates atmospheric depth, sky with cloud formations

Depth style: **atmospheric recession through progressive softening and mist**

## 5. Motion
- Static water surface suggests complete stillness
- Spanish moss implies potential for gentle breeze
- Vertical moss strands create downward visual pull
- Compositional flow follows water corridor into depth
- Cloud formations suggest atmospheric movement above
- Reflections anchor vertical elements to horizontal plane

## 6. Technique
- Fan brush for Spanish moss in delicate downward strokes
- Palette knife for tree trunk texture and bark detail
- 2-inch brush for foliage masses using push-up technique
- Wet-on-wet blending for atmospheric sky
- Liquid white/clear base for water reflections
- Liner brush for fine moss tendrils and hanging elements
- Light touch for reflections pulled straight down into water
Signature technique: **Spanish moss created with fan brush dragging**

## 7. Narrative Layer
"A secluded Southern wetland where time seems suspended beneath the weight of Spanish moss. The overcast sky casts no shadows, rendering the scene timeless and dreamlike. Autumn colors glow softly against the muted atmosphere, suggesting a place of quiet solitude far from human presence. The still water mirrors the trees above, doubling the sense of enclosure and creating a sanctuary within the wilderness."
